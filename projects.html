Andrew Taverna's Projects

Hockey Disc Challenge

This is my first ever iPhone App. This simple, but fun game, allows you to play a game in three different challenging modes. The game is currently in version 1.1. In the next 6 months, the game should have all the NHL team colors as an option for puck colors. Don't get too excited though, you have to unlock custom pucks by beating one of the three challenges!

Living Aboard Supplies

Living Aboard Supplies is a dropshop e-commerce store. The store is currently run on Magento and generally serves as a test store for any e-commerce projects I am working on. This store provides over 4,000 products that a liveaboard could need. This project has a very personal connection because one day I dream of living on my boat.

Producer Jungle

Producer Jungle launched in April of 2015 and is dedicated to breaking the barrier put up my the music industry. The goal is to connect as many talented audio eingineers to as many talented musicians as possible.
